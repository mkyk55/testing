Atomic design - An atom is the basic unit of an element. An atom is a form of matter which may not be further broken down using any chemical means. In Design - You first consider what you need to build your interface. From there, you start by creating the smallest meaningful items, atoms. When all of the atoms are built, you turn your attention to the slightly bigger meaningful items until you have a molecule. Once you’ve combined several molecules, you’ll be able to create a more complex structure, an organism.

Making Products Accessible for everyone

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are a part of a series of web accessibility guidelines published by the Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI) of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), the main international standards organization for the Internet. They are basically a set of recommendations for making Web content more accessible, primarily for people with disabilities.

Mostly websites are built with normal human beings in mind. But WHO report says that about 15% of the world's population lives with some of the disability? When we start to think, we get many questions in our mind. How will a blind person access a website? How will a person with hearing impairment be able to hear an audio or a live speech shown in a website? To cater to all these questions, we have a set of Accessibility Guidelines which guide us to build websites keeping everyone in mind.

When we follow all these Accessibility Guidelines, even the people with disabilities will be able to access the website with ease. For further reading, click source. Every website should be Perceivable, Operable, Understandable and Robust (POUR)according to the international standards by WCAG. WCAG has a set of guidelines to achieve the products/application that is,

Perceivable

Operable

Understandable

And Robust

At Ramboll EcoDesign Design, we are aiming to build 100% accessible products/applications by keeping everyone in mind.

Environmental Sustainability

Ramboll EcoDesign is kind to the environment. It aims to contribute to Environmental Sustainability by building components that are significantly lighter to process and store, hence leaving a lower carbon footprint on the environment. Design and development frameworks within Ramboll EcoDesign system helps to increase efficiency of energy usage by servers to process data.

Economic Sustainability

Ramboll EcoDesign serves as a building ground for faster launch times of digital products especially catered to produce robust, seamless, and happy experiences for varied user needs. It reduces costs and saves energy by using reusable components for our digital products.

Social Sustainability

Ramboll EcoDesign allows harmonious collaboration between teams who have the power to express their creativity in providing solutions that align in resonance with the user's needs and expectations. Hence it helps Ramboll to create a unified across all digital products. The components are designed to keep users with visual (inclusivity) and age-related disabilities in mind.
